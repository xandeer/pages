Trailing Whitespace

links
Emacs

ws-butler will be conflicted with auto-save. It will delete the current line's trailing whitespace.

What need to do next?

Disable auto-save in capture-buffer. 

(add-hook 'org-capture-mode-hook 'auto-save-disable)
(add-hook 'org-capture-prepare-finalize-hook 'auto-save-enable)
