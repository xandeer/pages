Read with a pen in hand

from
Read for Understanding

随时记录想法与Resonant, 过段时间肯定会忘记。

The idea is not to copy, but to have a meaningful dialogue with the texts we read.

Luhmann's reading workflow: "I always have a slip of paper at hand, on which I note down the ideas of certain pages. On the backside I write down the bibliographic details. After finishing the book I go through my notes and think how these notes might be relevant for already written notes in the slip-box. It means that I always read with an eye towards possible connections in the slip-box."

每个想法一条笔记，而不是按照原书的结构记录。

Writing by hand facilitates understanding.
